Pain is pleuritic, sharp, worse with deep inspiration. She reports mild hemoptysis (streaked blood in sputum) twice. She was recently started on oral contraceptives 2 months ago. PMH: obesity (BMI 34), factor V Leiden heterozygous (diagnosed 5 years ago, no prior thrombotic events). Family history: mother had DVT at age 50. Vitals: BP 132/84, HR 112, RR 22, SpO2 92% on room air, Temp 99.1F. Exam: tachycardic, clear lungs, mild right calf swelling and tenderness. D-dimer 4.2 (normal <0.5). ABG: pH 7.48, pCO2 30, pO2 68. ECG: sinus tachycardia, S1Q3T3 pattern, right axis deviation.
